Starbucks Copycat Honey Oatmilk Latte

Description

Now you can make this Starbucks copycat honey oatmilk latte at home for a fraction of the price, and you donâ€™t even have to go pick it up. It's made with only 3 ingredients that you can easily keep on hand.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup oat milk
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup hot coffee
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

Steps

  1. Place oat milk and honey in a tall, microwave-safe cup and heat until hot, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir until well combined. Froth hot oat milk mixture with an immersion blender or with a frothing wand.
  2. Pour coffee into a cup. Using the back of a spoon pour frothed milk over coffee. Pour foam over frothed milk. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
